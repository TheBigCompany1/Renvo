About this home

Stunning Remodeled Spanish Bungalow on an oversized 40 x 140 SF lot with a massive back yard for room to grow! From the moment you walk into this adorable vintage home you will fall in love with its attention to detail, charm and warmth. The great room boasts beautiful hardwood floors and encompasses a cozy living room with fireplace, dining area, and stylish remodeled chef's kitchen with center kitchen island that opens seamlessly to a large deck and patio in the backyard perfect for al fresco dining. The spacious light and bright primary bedroom boasts an en-suite luxury bathroom and has pristine views and direct access through French glass doors to the back yard oasis. A central focus of the backyard is the outdoor lounge area perfectly framed with trendy cafe style outdoor lights creating a warm ambience to entertain friends and family. Combining this area with the adjacent outdoor dining deck conveniently serviced by the adjacent kitchen, the outdoor entertaining options in this home are endless and effortless! Passing beyond the outdoor lounge lies a private fenced massive grass lawn perfect for your kids and dogs to safely play! Finally, there is a detached garage / bonus room area that could be the perfect play room for kids, home office, home gym, or lounge, with tons of extra coveted storage and laundry hook ups. This structure could also be converted to an ADU. The future expansion opportunities to this move in ready charming Spanish bungalow sited on such a large and deep lot allow this home to easily grow with your family!
