Learn More About Renvo

Renvo specializes in helping homeowners and investors identify the Highest and Best Use (HBU) of their properties. From small remodels to major construction projects, we show you where to invest to maximize your ROI.

How We Do It

  • Collect real estate market data (recent sales, rental comps, and trends).
  • Analyze which improvements—extra bedrooms, pool, ADU—can increase a property’s value or rental income.
  • Offer data-driven recommendations for your property’s HBU.

Why HBU Matters

Highest and Best Use is a standard in real estate valuation, ensuring you’re not leaving money on the table. By aligning your property’s use with the market’s demand, you can significantly boost profitability.
