Frequently Asked Questions

What is Highest and Best Use (HBU)?

HBU is a real estate valuation concept that identifies the most profitable or productive use of a property, irrespective of its current use. By aligning with HBU, you can often unlock higher returns.

How does Renvo help determine HBU?

We combine local market data, real-time comps, and advanced analytics to see where your property’s potential lies—be it an ADU addition, a rental conversion, or a remodel. We then present the top options based on ROI and feasibility.

Do I need a real estate license to use Renvo?

No, Renvo is open to homeowners, prospective buyers, and investors. We offer easy-to-understand insights without requiring professional licensing.
